About Me

Academic Background

I am currently pursuing my degree in communication with a focus on public interest communication and strategic messaging. I enjoy courses that blend research, writing, and design, especially those that explore how campaigns can create positive social impact.

In team projects, I often take the lead on organizing timelines, clarifying goals, and transforming research into clear messages and visuals. I value professionalism, reliability, and strong collaboration skills.

Alongside my coursework, I continue building my skills in digital tools such as Adobe Creative Cloud, basic HTML/CSS, and content management platforms. These skills help me connect strategic thinking with practical execution.

I am committed to ethical communication, transparency, and audience-centered storytelling—principles that guide my academic work and future career interests.
